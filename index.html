About Me


Hey, I’m Hitakshi! I’m currently studying Computer Science at VIT Vellore, diving deep into Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I’ve always been curious about how things work—especially when it comes to tech. That curiosity turned into passion the day I built my first program… and crashed it five times before it worked!


I love creating things that are useful and meaningful. Whether it’s designing clean, responsive websites or figuring out how to make an algorithm smarter, the problem-solving process excites me. I'm also a bit of a multitasker—I’ve been involved in everything from organizing tech events as Co-Secretary of SIAM-VIT to writing for our student newsletter.


Working on real projects and leading teams has taught me way more than textbooks ever could. I’ve learned to stay calm under pressure, listen better, and genuinely enjoy collaborating with people.


When I’m not coding or running events, you’ll probably find me exploring new tools, rewatching sci-fi movies, or jotting down random ideas for passion projects. I don’t have it all figured out yet, but I’m excited to keep learning, building, and growing—one line of code at a time.
